Medicine Bow Outfitters

 
 
 
 
 




Deer Hunting in North Park has always proven to be some of the best in the State! Good management techniques, along with limited access, have helped in maintaining this high quality deer heard. Our entire hunting area contains excellent deer habitat, from Willow Lined River Bottoms, to miles of Sage Brush with Hidden Drainages that lead up through Big Quacky Aspen and Pine Timbered Draws which start well above Timber Line. It all holds deer, and it’s all within reach from our Remote Base Camp. Another aspect of a quality deer herd is Genetics. Take a look at some of the great deer featured below that have been apart of this great deer herd.

Deer tags for our units are by Drawing Only, which means you must apply for a license.  It takes several preference points to draw for the later Rifle Seasons, although there is the occasional “lucky duck” who gets it the first time. So start applying today. You can apply online at www.cpw.state.co.us after mid-February and the deadline is April 1st. You can also ask about the availability of our Voucher Tags. For Season Dates and license fees click on, http://cpw.state.co.us/Hunting/SeasonDatesAndFees/BigGameDatesAndFees.htm

Ask about our Greater Sage Grouse & Muzzleloader Deer Combo Hunt!
